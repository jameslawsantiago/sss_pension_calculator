üßÆ How It Works

The SSS pension is calculated using three different formulas, and you receive the highest amount among them. Our calculator uses your contribution history and years of service to determine your benefits.

üìà Maximize Your Pension

To get the highest possible pension, maximize your contributions during the last 5 years before retirement. Your pension is based on your salary credits for this period.

üí° Additional Benefits
